## FAQ

### What kind of company is Venture Electronics?
Venture Electronics is a China-based manufacturing partner for PCB Manufacturing, PCB Assembly and EMS-related project discussions with overseas electronics buyers.

### Is Venture Electronics only a PCBA supplier?
No. PCB Assembly / PCBA is the primary conversion service, while fabrication, sourcing, DFM, testing and EMS or Box Build support may be included according to the project.

### Does Venture operate every process in one owned factory?
The public positioning is manufacturing partner and project-based manufacturing support. Individual resources, facilities and certificates should be described only from approved evidence.

### What project sizes can be discussed?
Prototype, low-volume and repeat-production needs can be reviewed. Quantity, material, process setup, testing and schedule are evaluated per project rather than through one universal MOQ statement.

### Where can I verify Venture's official websites and contact details?
Use the Official Resources and Contact pages, which identify the current main website, legacy PCBA-focused asset and confirmed inquiry details.

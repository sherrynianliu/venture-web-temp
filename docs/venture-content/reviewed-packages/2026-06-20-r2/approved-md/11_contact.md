## FAQ

### Which email should I use?
Use `info@venture-mfg.com` for current company and project inquiries.

### Is there a public WhatsApp or WeChat account?
No account should be displayed until the number or account, ownership and public-use approval are confirmed.

### Where should I send large or confidential project files?
Start by email and ask for the preferred transfer route. NDA terms and the file-transfer method should be agreed before sensitive materials are shared.

### When should I use Request a Quote instead of Contact?
Use Request a Quote when board files, BOM, quantities, drawings or testing requirements are ready. Contact is better for general questions and early fit checks.

### Region 3 — Fabrication-to-Assembly Relationship

- **H2:** Why should assembly requirements be reviewed before PCB fabrication?

Pad design, solder-mask openings, surface finish, panelization, fiducials, via-in-pad, board thickness and test-point access can affect SMT, through-hole assembly, inspection and testing. When fabrication is part of a Turnkey PCBA scope, the bare-board and assembly requirements should be reviewed together rather than as unrelated purchases.

### Region 4 — Specification Categories

- **H2:** Which PCB specification categories can be reviewed?

| Category | Review focus |
|---|---|
| Board type | Rigid, flex or rigid-flex requirement and project fit |
| Stack-up and material | Layer structure, FR-4 or other stated material, Tg and impedance needs |
| Copper and geometry | Copper weight, line or spacing requirement and current-carrying considerations |
| Holes and vias | Mechanical, laser, blind, buried or via-in-pad requirements where applicable |
| Surface finish | Finish selection and downstream assembly or storage considerations |
| Panelization | Manufacturing efficiency, fiducials, rails, depaneling and assembly handling |
| Inspection and electrical test | Required bare-board checks and records confirmed by project |

A current approved capability table is required before publishing numeric limits for layer count, dimensions, line or spacing, holes, impedance tolerance or special processes.

## FAQ

### What is the difference between PCB fabrication and PCB Assembly?
PCB fabrication produces the bare printed circuit board. PCB Assembly places and solders electronic components onto the bare board and then applies the agreed inspection or testing scope.

### What files are needed for a PCB fabrication quote?
Gerber or ODB++, stack-up, material, thickness, copper, impedance, finish, size, panelization, hole and quantity information are typical inputs. Assembly constraints should also be shared when the board will proceed into PCBA.

### Can PCB fabrication be included in a Turnkey PCBA project?
Yes. A turnkey discussion may coordinate bare-board fabrication, approved component sourcing, assembly, inspection, testing and packaging under one quotation.

### Can Venture publish a full numeric capability table?
A numeric table should be published only after a current capability source is approved. Until then, specific limits are reviewed from the project files.

### Can special materials or via structures be reviewed?
They can be discussed when the material, stack-up, hole or via requirement and downstream assembly needs are stated. Final feasibility is project-specific.

### Why do assembly requirements matter before fabrication?
Board finish, pads, panelization, fiducials, via design and test points can affect placement, soldering, inspection, testing and depaneling.

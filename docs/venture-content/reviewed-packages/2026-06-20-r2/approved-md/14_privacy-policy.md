## Reviewed public legal copy
**Last updated: June 20, 2026**

### 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information received through the Venture Electronics website, direct email communications sent to `info@venture-mfg.com`, and related business discussions initiated from the website.

For this policy, “Venture Electronics,” “Venture,” “we,” “us” or “our” refers to Venture Electronics Technology Ltd, also identified in Chinese as 威驰科技有限公司.

This website is intended for business-to-business information and inquiries. The first website release does not provide user accounts, online payments or a connected web file-upload form.

### 2. Information you choose to provide

You may provide information when you contact Venture or request a project review, including:

- Your name, job title, company and business contact details
- Email address and telephone number
- Inquiry content and communication history
- Gerber or ODB++ files, BOMs, CPL or pick-and-place data, assembly drawings and project specifications
- Mechanical, cable, test, firmware, label, packaging or delivery information
- NDA requests, quotation information and other business documents
- Any other information you choose to include in an email or business communication

Do not send personal information that is not reasonably needed for the business inquiry.

### 3. Technical information

When you visit the website, hosting, security or network-service providers may process standard technical information, such as:

- Internet Protocol address
- Browser and device type
- Requested page, date and time
- Referring page
- Basic server, security and error logs

This information may be used to deliver the website, maintain security, diagnose errors and understand general website performance.

If analytics, advertising cookies, a connected form or another tracking service is added later, this policy and the website’s consent treatment should be reviewed before that service is enabled.

### 4. How information may be used

Venture may use information to:

- Respond to an inquiry
- Review whether a PCB, PCBA, sourcing, testing or EMS project can be discussed
- Identify missing files or project requirements
- Prepare, discuss or revise a quotation
- Communicate about BOM, DFM, sourcing, testing, packaging or delivery questions
- Protect the website, systems and business communications
- Maintain reasonable business, project and legal records
- Comply with applicable legal or regulatory requirements
- Establish, exercise or defend legal rights

Information provided for a project review is not used to grant permission for Venture to publish the buyer’s identity, files, product or project as marketing content.

### 5. Sharing information

Venture may share information only where reasonably necessary for website operation, business communication, project evaluation, the requested manufacturing scope, professional advice, security or legal compliance.

Recipients may include:

- Website hosting, email, security or information-technology providers
- Professional advisers
- Service providers or manufacturing resources involved in evaluating or performing an approved project scope
- Government, regulatory or legal authorities when disclosure is required or legally appropriate
- A successor organization in connection with a business transaction, subject to applicable safeguards

Project-sensitive information should be shared according to the applicable NDA, quotation, purchase-order terms, quality agreement or manufacturing agreement.

Venture does not authorize public use of a buyer’s name, logo, files or project details merely because the buyer submitted an inquiry.

### 6. International processing

Venture Electronics is based in Shenzhen, China, and may communicate with buyers and service providers in other countries. Information may therefore be processed in a country different from the country where it was provided.

Where an applicable law requires a particular transfer safeguard or contract, that requirement should be addressed in the relevant business or data-processing arrangement.

### 7. Retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably needed to:

- Respond to and manage the inquiry
- Evaluate or perform the requested project
- Maintain quotation, project, quality, financial or legal records
- Resolve disputes
- Meet applicable legal or contractual requirements

Retention periods may differ according to the information and project. A buyer may contact Venture to ask about deletion or another privacy request, subject to legal, contractual, security and record-keeping obligations.

### 8. Security and confidential project files

Venture uses reasonable organizational and technical measures intended to protect business information. No internet or email transmission method is completely secure.

Before sending highly sensitive design files, source code, firmware, personal information or other confidential material:

- Ask whether an NDA is needed
- Confirm the correct recipient and company entity
- Agree on an appropriate transfer method
- Remove personal information that is not needed for the project

A project-specific NDA or written agreement, not this general website policy, controls the detailed confidentiality obligations for the project.

### 9. Your privacy choices

Depending on applicable law, you may have rights or choices regarding access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection or another form of control over personal information.

To make a request, email `info@venture-mfg.com` and describe:

- Who you are
- The information or communication involved
- The request you are making
- Any information reasonably needed to verify and locate the record

Venture may need to verify the request and may retain information when required for a contract, legal obligation, security or dispute.

### 10. Third-party websites

The website may link to another website or service. Venture does not control the privacy practices of an independent third party. Review the third party’s privacy information before providing data through that service.

### 11. Children

This website is directed to business and professional users and is not designed for children. Venture does not knowingly seek personal information from children through the website.

### 12. Changes to this policy

Venture may update this policy when the website, inquiry process, service providers or legal requirements change. The revised version should show a new “Last updated” date.
